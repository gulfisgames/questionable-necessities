Keep your table's house rules and rulings within easy reach. Each rule is a card showing its title and summary, with the full details one click away.

##### Create a Rule

Click **Add Rule** and fill in a title, a summary for quick reference at the table, and the full details. Click **Save** to keep your work. Summaries and details support Markdown formatting.

##### Read and Edit

Click a card to open the rule. Switch between **Preview** and **Edit** to read or change it, and click **Save**. Closing a rule without saving puts it back the way it was.

##### Organise

Use the arrows on a card to move it up or down the list. Use the search bar to find a rule by its title, summary or details; close matches count too. Clear the search to move cards again.

##### Delete

Open a rule and click **Delete**. You will be asked to confirm, because this cannot be undone.

##### Back Up and Share

Rules are saved in your browser. Use **Export Rules JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Rules JSON** to restore them or load rules shared with you.
