Track how each character's choices shift their alignment over the course of your campaign.

##### Add Characters

Type a name and click **Add Character**. Each character gets a coloured marker on the chart and a matching row in the list. Names must be unique and at least two letters long.

##### Adjust Alignment

Drag a character's marker across the chart as their behaviour evolves: up is good, down is evil, right is lawful and left is chaotic.

The list shows each character's current alignment, such as Chaotic Good. Hover over it to spot that character on the chart.

##### Rename and Remove

Click a name in the list to edit it. Swipe a row to the left to reveal **Delete** and remove the character.

##### Back Up and Share

Characters are saved in your browser. Use **Export Characters JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Characters JSON** to restore it or load a party shared with you.
