Print cards for the magic items and treasures for your tabletop games. Each card shows the name, type, rarity, attunement requirement, and description.

##### Create an Item Card

Fill in the item's identifier, name, type, rarity, attunement requirement, and description, then click **Save**. Click **Clear** to discard the current item and start fresh.

##### Edit an Item Card

Switch to **Table** view to see all your cards. Click a row to load the item into the editor; make your changes and click **Save**.

##### Delete an Item Card

Swipe a row to the left to reveal **Delete** and remove the card.

##### Adjust Size

In **Table** view, use the size selector on each row to set the print size of that card.

##### Card Sets

Choose a preset collection at the bottom of the editor to load a ready-made group of items. Click **Clear Items** to start fresh.

##### Print Item Cards

After adding your items cards,

1. Switch to **PDF** view to see your cards as they will appear on the page.
2. Click **Export Item Cards PDF** to download the print sheet.
3. Open it in a PDF viewer and print on A4 paper. Adjust margins to suit your printer for the best result.

##### Back Up and Share

In **Table** view, use **Export Item Cards JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Item Cards JSON** to restore them or load cards shared with you.
