Print circular combat tokens for your tabletop games. Sized from Tiny to Gargantuan and framed by a coloured border.

##### Build a Token

1. Paste an image URL.
2. Pick a size.
3. Choose a border colour.
4. Fit the image using the zoom and position controls, or drag and scroll the image directly.
5. Click **Add 1 Token** or **Add 8 Tokens** to save the combat token.

##### Token Sets

Choose a preset collection — Demons, Devils, Fiends, and more — to load a ready-made group.

##### Remove a Token

Click any token in the sheet to remove it.

##### Print Sheets

Added tokens are displayed in the print sheet on the right.
When you are happy with the set,

1. Click **Export Combat Tokens HTML** to download it.
2. Open the file in a browser.
3. Print on A4 paper. Enable background graphics in the print settings and adjust margins to suit your printer for the best result.

##### Back Up and Share

Use **Export Combat Tokens JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Combat Tokens JSON** to restore them or load tokens shared with you.
