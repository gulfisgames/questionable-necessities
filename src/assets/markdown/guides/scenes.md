Prepare your sessions as a list of scenes, each with a date, a title, a short description and detailed notes.

##### Create a Scene

Click **Add Scene**. A fresh scene opens at the top of the list, dated for the coming Saturday. Click the date field to pick another day from the calendar.

##### Edit and Save

Click a scene's heading to open it. Switch between **Preview** and **Edit** to read or change it, and click **Save** to keep your work. Notes support Markdown formatting, so headings and lists display nicely in the preview.

Closing a scene without saving puts it back the way it was, and a brand-new scene left unchanged is removed when closed.

##### Organise

Drag the handle beside a scene to reorder the list. Use the search bar to find scenes by title, date or notes; close matches count too. Clear the search to reorder again.

##### Delete

Open a scene and click **Delete**. You will be asked to confirm, because this cannot be undone.

##### Back Up and Share

Scenes are saved in your browser. Use **Export Scenes JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Scenes JSON** to restore them or load scenes shared with you.
