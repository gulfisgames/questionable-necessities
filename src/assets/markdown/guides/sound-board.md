Build a sound board of YouTube videos, ready to play at the click of a button.

##### Build Your Board

Click **Add Sound Group** to create a group, give each sound a name and a YouTube link. Use **Add Sound** to add more; a group holds up to six sounds. You can add as many groups as you like.

##### Play

Each sound appears as a coloured button on its group's row. Click the button to play, and click it again to pause. Sounds repeat until paused.

##### Player Controls

The bar at the top shows what is playing. Use it to pause and resume, skip 15 seconds back or forward, adjust the volume, or click the progress bar to jump to a moment. The keyboard works too: the space bar plays and pauses, and the left and right arrow keys skip.

##### Organise

Drag the handle beside a group to reorder your board. To remove a sound, open the group and click the delete button beside it, or choose **Delete Sound Group** to remove the whole group.

##### Back Up and Share

Your board is saved in your browser. Use **Export Sound Board JSON** to download a copy, and **Import Sound Board JSON** to restore it or load a board shared with you.
